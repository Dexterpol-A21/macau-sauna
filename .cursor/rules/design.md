# Design Rules — Macau Sauna Booking (redesign-test)

## Palette — Neon Red Nightlife

**Fondos:** `#0A0A0A`, `#111111`
**Texto:** `#FFF8F0` / muted `#9E9E9E`

### Brand
- **Primary:** `#FF2D55`
- **Light:** `#FF6B85`
- **Dark:** `#C41E3A`
- **Accent:** `#FF4D6A`

No gold. No mint/cyan.

### CTAs
- Primary: gradient `#C41E3A` → `#FF2D55` → `#FF6B85`, text `#FFF8F0` (white) for contrast on red
- Secondary outline: border/text `#FF2D55`

## Tipografía
- **Noto Sans TC** for headings and body

## Reglas
- English only
- No em-dashes in copy
